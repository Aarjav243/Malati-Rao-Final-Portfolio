About

Two decades of documentary filmmaking — from Tibetan monasteries to Indian prisons, from vanishing craft traditions to questions of identity, rights, and belonging. Award-winning films broadcast nationally and internationally. Currently directing two feature-length documentaries on mental health and marriage equality in India.
