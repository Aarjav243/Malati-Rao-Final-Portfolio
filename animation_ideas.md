# Malati Rao Portfolio — Motion Design Animation Ideas

This document tracks various GSAP-driven animation options for each section of the portfolio. Choose one from each category to implement.

---

## 🏠 Home Hero (The Big Title)
- **A. Collision (Current):** "Malati" drops from top, "Rao" rises from bottom. They meet.
- **B. Typewriter:** Letters appear one-by-one as if being typed live on screen.
- **C. Blur-to-Sharp:** The entire title starts completely blurry and slowly comes into crystal-clear focus.
- **D. Scramble:** Letters show random characters flickering rapidly before locking into the correct word — like a code cracking.
- **E. Horizontal Stretch:** Words start very wide and squashed, then snap to their normal shape with a spring bounce.
- **F. Split Curtain:** A gold line divides the screen in half. "Malati" slides in from the left half, "Rao" from the right.

---

## 🎬 Films List (The numbered rows)
- **A. Triple Slide (Current):** Numbers drop from top, titles slide left, dates slide right.
- **B. Film Strip Roll:** Each row rolls in like a strip of film, with a slight rotation that settles flat.
- **C. Ink Reveal:** A gold line sweeps left-to-right across each row, "painting" it into existence.
- **D. Accordion Drop:** The entire list drops from above as if the page is a folded accordion being opened.
- **E. Flicker Load:** Each row flashes/flickers 2–3 times before solidifying, like an old projector warming up.
- **F. Elastic Bounce:** Each row slides in from the left but slightly overshoots and bounces back to position.

---

## 🎞 Individual Film Page (Title & Details)
- **A. Clip Wipe (Current):** Title is hidden behind a mask that slides upward to reveal it.
- **B. Vertical Split:** The film title splits into top and bottom halves that slide apart to reveal themselves.
- **C. Parallax Depth:** The title and the video embed move at slightly different speeds as the page loads, creating a 3D depth feel.
- **D. Spotlight:** Page starts dark. A spotlight slowly brightens to reveal the title from the center outward.
- **E. Word by Word:** Each word of the title fades and rises in sequence — like reading a title card in a cinema.
- **F. Glitch:** The title glitches (shifts sideways 2–3 times rapidly) before locking into place — cinematic and edgy.

---

## 👤 Bio Page (Text & CV Entries)
- **A. Fade Up (Current):** Paragraphs drift upward and fade in as you scroll.
- **B. Line by Line:** Each sentence slides up individually with a tiny delay, making you read the bio as a story.
- **C. Timeline Draw:** A vertical gold line draws downward through the CV entries as you scroll, connecting each one.
- **D. Flip Cards:** Each CV entry flips from invisible to visible like flipping a card face-up.
- **E. Sticky Heading:** The section heading (e.g., "Education") stays fixed while its entries animate in below it.
- **F. Scale from Center:** Each CV row scales up from 90% to 100% as it enters the viewport — subtle but elegant.

---

## 📰 Press & Interviews Page
- **A. Fade Scale (Current):** Items fade in and grow slightly from 98% to 100%.
- **B. Magazine Flip:** Each press item enters with a slight horizontal rotation (like turning a magazine page).
- **C. Gold Underline Draw:** As each article title appears, a gold underline draws itself from left to right beneath it.
- **D. Cascade Drop:** Articles fall in from above in a waterfall, each one slightly delayed after the previous.
- **E. Slide from Gutter:** Articles slide in from the far left margin (like newspaper columns coming onto the page).
- **F. Stagger Fade Grid:** All items are invisible; they pulse into view in a diagonal wave pattern across the grid.

---

## 📬 Contact Page
- **A. Fade Up (Current):** Contact blocks drift up and fade in.
- **B. Letter Drop:** The email address appears letter-by-letter, as if being written out.
- **C. Gold Border Grow:** A gold border draws itself around the contact card from the top-left corner, clockwise.
- **D. Pulse:** The email/contact links pulse with a soft gold glow once when they appear.
- **E. Slide-in Panel:** The entire contact section slides in from the right edge of the screen like a drawer opening.
- **F. Number Count:** Any statistics or info counts upward from 0 to its final value as you scroll into view.
