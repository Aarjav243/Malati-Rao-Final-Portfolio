Setu — The Bridge

A film about Mumbai's prison system

Film available on request

Setu — The Bridge is a documentary film examining Mumbai's prison system. Produced by Prayas TISS Mumbai and the Azim Premji Foundation, the film is part of Malati Rao's sustained engagement with incarceration, rehabilitation, and the lives of those caught within the carceral system.
