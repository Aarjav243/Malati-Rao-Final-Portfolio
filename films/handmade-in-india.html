Handmade in India

A documentary on India's living craft traditions

India carries one of the world's richest craft traditions — passed down through families and communities over centuries. Handmade in India travels across the country to meet the artisans who keep these traditions alive: weavers, potters, metalworkers, embroiderers, and makers of forms both ancient and evolving.

The film is both a celebration and an elegy — acknowledging the beauty and precision of handmade work while asking what it means to sustain a craft in a world that increasingly does not reward slowness or intricacy. Produced by the Public Diplomacy Division of the Ministry of External Affairs, Government of India.
