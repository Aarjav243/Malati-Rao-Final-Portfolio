Hopes and Fears is a feature-length documentary chronicling the historic legal battle for marriage equality in India. Following the petitioners, lawyers, and advocates who brought the case before the Supreme Court of India in 2022–2023, the film captures the intimate human stories behind a landmark constitutional moment.

The film is currently in post-production.
