Funeral in my Brain

A feature-length documentary series on Mental Health in India

Post-Production

Currently in
Post-Production

Funeral in my Brain is a feature-length documentary series examining mental health in India — the lived experiences of individuals navigating the mental health system, the families who support them, and the practitioners working within a system under strain.

The title draws from Emily Dickinson's poem, evoking the interior landscape of psychological distress. Related research by Malati Rao was presented at the Indian Association of Social Psychiatry Conference, New Delhi, in 2022. The film is currently in post-production.
