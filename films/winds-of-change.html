Winds of Change

A film about marriage equality

Completed

Film available through
NHK Japan

Winds of Change is a documentary film exploring marriage equality — the legal, personal, and social dimensions of the fight for the right to marry for same-sex couples in India. Produced by NHK, Japan, the film was directed between August 2024 and January 2025.
